Areas of Expertise

Comprehensive, world-class care in Otorhinolaryngology, specializing in advanced surgical procedures and patient-centered treatment plans.

Rhinology & Endoscopic Sinus Surgery

Advanced Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery (FESS) and treatment of complex sinus disorders, nasal polyps, and chronic rhinosinusitis.

Head & Neck Surgery

Expert surgical management of benign and malignant tumors of the head and neck, including thyroid, parathyroid, and salivary gland surgeries.

Otology & Hearing Restoration

Treatment of middle ear infections, tympanoplasty, hearing loss management, and assistance in advanced cochlear implantation procedures.

Pediatric ENT

Compassionate care for children, including minimally invasive tonsillectomies (using BiZact technology), adenoidectomies, and airway management.

Laryngology & Voice

Diagnosis and treatment of vocal cord disorders, hoarseness, swallowing difficulties, and comprehensive airway reconstruction.

Facial Trauma & Emergency

Certified in Advanced Trauma Life Support (ATLS), providing expert surgical reconstruction and care for complex facial and ENT emergencies.
